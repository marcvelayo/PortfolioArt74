Project 3- Minecraft

I chose the Jollibee logo as my Minecraft object. It represents how Filipino Culture is heavily integrated within the United States. It is the most famous Filipino fast food chain, and has numerous locations around the nation. It also represents how fast food places are a big part of the American culture.
